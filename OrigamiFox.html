Origami Fox

Step 1. Fold the square in half

Start by folding the lower left corner of your square up to meet the upper right corner of your square. You should be left with a triangle.




Step 2. Fold two corners in to make a square

Fold the left corner of your triangle in to meet the upper right corner (the right angle). Do the same with the lower corner of your triangle.
You should be left with a small square with a crease diagonally down the middle where the two triangles meet.




Step 3. Flip and fold the square in half

Flip your square over so that all of the folds are facing away from you.
Keeping your square in the same position, fold the upper right corner down to meet the lower left corner. Be sure to match the corners up precisely before creasing your fold.




Step 4. Fold the left side over, Unfold the rectangle

Fold the entire left side of your triangle over to the right. Try to fold it so that the straight edge will intersect the remaining triangle in half. You will notice that the rectangle on the left side of your piece is comprised of two folds. Starting at the bottom of the rectangle, separate these two sections. Fold the top fold to the right and the bottom fold to the left.

<



Step 5. Finish up your Fox

To finish your fox, encourage the head to fold down by running your fingers along the crease. You can also fold the tail out to help it stand up. Lastly, you may decorate your fox's face in whatever way you choose.
You can draw on a nose and eyes, or use googly eyes to give your fox its expression.
